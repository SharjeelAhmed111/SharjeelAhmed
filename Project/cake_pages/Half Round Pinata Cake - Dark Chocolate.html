Say Hello To The Piñata Cake: The Smashing Trend

Celebrations are incomplete without cake. A cake is a must-have for every celebration. Be it a birthday, surprise party, anniversary, or any other special event, your cake must steal the show. The Piñata cake or Smash cake is one of the most popular celebrations and cake fads in the world right now.
Around Valentine's Day, 2021, Piñata cakes first appeared on Instagram, built using a heart-shaped mould which was all the rage. The Piñata cake's purpose is to spice up the traditional cake-cutting ritual by adding a new element of surprise and curiosity.
Piñata cakes bring a "wow" factor to keep people wondering about what's inside. The cake-cutting ceremony is made more fascinating and intricate by smashing open a Piñata cake with a hammer and cutting it into pieces. It's all inspired by the Piñata tradition!
Some people appreciate the full comedic spectacle of smashing the Piñata in a few strokes with the cake cutter blindfolded.
